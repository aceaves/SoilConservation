The approach used here includes the use of spatially-explicit predictive models, Scenario Planning, and System Dynamics through Vensim. 
It outlines a method available to evaluate the effectiveness of soil conservation programmes to control landslides. Soil conservation modelling explores future scenarios to mitigate soil loss from farms through pole planting and native reversion corrections against a reference scenario of 'minimal correction’. It compares the medium-term economic costs and environmental impacts of targeted planting against the long-term gains from conserving soil in situ.
